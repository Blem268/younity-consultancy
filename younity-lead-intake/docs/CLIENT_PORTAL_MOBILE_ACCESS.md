# Client Portal Mobile Access

Clients can add the Younity Client Portal to a phone home screen. The icon opens the secure client login page at `/client/login`; clients still need their email address and password. This is not downloaded from the App Store.

## iPhone

1. Open the client portal in Safari.
2. Tap Share.
3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
4. Tap Add.
5. Use the Younity icon to open the portal.

## Android

1. Open the client portal in Chrome.
2. Tap the menu.
3. Tap Add to Home screen or Install app.
4. Tap Add or Install.
5. Use the Younity icon to open the portal.
